Exploring Opportunity

This ongoing project creates a client-side web application for researchers to visualize trends of social mobility in the United States. It allows users to easily map, plot, and make OLS regression tables with data of the Equality of Opportunity project as well as their own local data.

I intend to add features to query the Census API, fit different types of functional forms, and the ability to conduct Principle Component Analyses.
