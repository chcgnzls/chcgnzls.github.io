Research Interests

My previous research documented growing disparity in the rates of tissue plasminogen activator (tPA) utilization between rural and urban hospitals and the effectiveness of Primary Stroke Center accreditation in closing the gap. I have also shown substantial variation in rates of health insurance coverage among different Latino groups, differences in the relationships with the likelihood of having health insurance with factors such as immigration status, and that substantial reductions in coverage disparities both among Latinos and between Latinos and Whites were concurrent with implementation of the Affordable Care Act (ACA) and Medicaid expansion.

Currently, I’m interested in identifying modern and historical sources of structural and implicit racism, particularly in the Southwest and West.
