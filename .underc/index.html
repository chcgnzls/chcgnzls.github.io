Exploring Opportunity

This ongoing project creates a client-side web application for researchers to visualize trends in Social Mobility in the United States. It allows users to map the data of the Equality of Opportunity project, query additional data from the U.S. Census Bureau, and upload their own data, and develop predictive models.
